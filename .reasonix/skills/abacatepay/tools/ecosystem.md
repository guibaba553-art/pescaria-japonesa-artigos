# AbacatePay Ecosystem

## What is the AbacatePay Ecosystem?

The AbacatePay ecosystem brings together **open source projects actively maintained by [almeida](https://github.com/almeidazs) and [albuquerque](https://links.albuquerquesz.com.br/), and the community** that expand, facilitate, or customize integration with our payment platform.

Here you find complementary SDKs, development tools, themes, plugins, and integrations with other frameworks and platforms.

### What You Find in the Ecosystem
- **Official Projects**: Maintained and supported by the AbacatePay team
- **Community Projects**: Created by developers who use AbacatePay daily
- **DX Tools**: CLIs, helpers, mocks, templates, and automations
- **Extensions and Plugins**: Integrations with frameworks, CMSs, and platforms
- **Themes and UI Kits**: Visual components to speed up frontend integrations

## Official Projects

Projects maintained directly by the AbacatePay team, with versioning, documentation, and continuous support.

- **Official SDKs**: Official libraries for integration with the AbacatePay API in various languages.
- **AbacatePay CLI**: Command-line tool for development, testing, and local automations.
- **Official Templates**: Ready templates to start projects with AbacatePay in different stacks.
- **Webhooks & Dev Tools**: Auxiliary tools for testing, simulating, and debugging API events.

## Community Projects

Initiatives created and maintained by the community that broaden the use of AbacatePay in different contexts.

> ⚠️ Community projects **are not officially maintained**, but many are widely used and recommended.

- **Framework Integrations**: Plugins and wrappers for backend and frontend frameworks.
- **CMS and E-commerce Plugins**: Integrations with platforms like CMSs, online stores, and management systems.
- **UI Kits & Components**: Ready visual components for checkout, payment status, and dashboards.
- **Testing & Mock Tools**: Mocks, simulators, and tools for automated testing.

## Want to Contribute?

Contributions are **very welcome**. You can:

- Create a new library or integration
- Improve existing documentation
- Report bugs or inconsistencies
- Share your project with the community

### How to Submit Your Project
Open an issue or pull request in the corresponding GitHub repository.
If you want to highlight your project here, contact the AbacatePay team.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's the difference between official and community projects?
Official projects are maintained by the AbacatePay team and have direct support.
Community projects are developed by third parties and may have varying levels of maintenance.

### Can I use community projects in production?
Yes, but we recommend evaluating maintenance level, tests, and compatibility with your stack beforehand.

### Can my project become official?
In some cases, yes. Well-adopted, well-documented projects aligned with the ecosystem can be incorporated or officially recommended.
