# AbacatePay FAQ 🥑

This FAQ gathers the most common questions about AbacatePay. If your question is not here yet, use the doubts channel: https://discord.com/channels/1303726278670553158/1303730939490336819.

1. **How to get support faster?**

If you are not answered quickly in the channel https://discord.com/channels/1303726278670553158/1303730939490336819, you can send an email to *help@abacatepay.com* or enter the support chat on the official website (*[abacatepay.com](https://www.abacatepay.com/))*.

2. **Does AbacatePay support bank slips and credit cards?**

From our *v2* you can use *Credit Card* and payments with *Bank Slips*, in addition to *PIX* that we have supported since v1.

3. **Can I reverse or refund a payment?**

We currently do not support reversals or refunds, but we plan to implement in future updates.

4. **Does AbacatePay support currencies other than Real?**

We do not support any currency other than Real (BRL), but we want to add as soon as possible.

5. **What is the PIX and credit card fee?**

Currently our fee for **all** PIX transactions is *R$ 0.80* cents, and *3.50%* + *R$ 0.60* per transaction for credit card.

6. **Where can I find the documentation or official GitHub?**

You can find our documentation at *[docs.abacatepay.com](https://docs.abacatepay.com)* and our Github organization at *[github.com/AbacatePay](https://github.com/AbacatePay)*.
